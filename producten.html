Focusing on "innovation driven" and "accessible to patients", through source innovation and cooperative introduction, we have built a rich product pipeline, including monoclonal antibodies, fusion proteins, small molecule drugs and other types, covering the treatment fields of anti-tumor, metabolic diseases, autoimmune diseases and nervous system diseases. We are in different research and development stages from marketing to early exploration and discovery.
With the continuous enrichment of product pipeline and further exploration of drug combination therapy, our innovation field will continue to expand to more types of drug research and development, including small molecule drugs, antibody drug conjugate (ADC), and the exploration of the next generation of innovative therapy for cancer and autoimmune diseases.
