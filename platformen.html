R & D Center

We are committed to the development of innovative and best in class macromolecular drugs through independent innovation.

We have established a globally integrated R & D process with three R & D centers all over the world. We have excellent ability to discover and develop innovative drugs. We can independently carry out target evaluation, mechanism research and validation, clinical drug screening and functional verification, and other key steps of drug discovery and development.
American R & D Center
San Francisco laboratory and Maryland laboratory mainly focus on the mechanism research of known and innovative targets in the field of cancer and autoimmune diseases, drug discovery and precise screening of drug molecules.
Laboratories in the United States pay close attention to the latest technological trends in the research and development of biotechnological innovative drugs.
